Escrow Protection

Secure payment flow
MSALINA Hub can hold buyer payment until the order is delivered or confirmed, helping reduce risk between buyer and seller.
1. Buyer places order Payment is initiated for the selected product or service.
2. Funds are held The amount stays protected while the seller prepares delivery.
3. Buyer confirms delivery After receiving the item/service, the buyer approves release.
4. Seller gets paid The escrow releases funds to the seller after confirmation.
